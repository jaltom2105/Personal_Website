My Journey

I am deeply fascinated by how human thought processes can be modeled and replicated within artificial systems. My dual background in Artificial Intelligence and Psychology allows me to approach problems from both a technical and a human-centric perspective.

At Purdue, I've focused on understanding the cognitive biases that influence decision-making and how these can be mitigated or utilized in machine learning models. I believe that the future of technology lies not just in power, but in "constructive functionality"—creating tools that are as intuitive as they are intelligent.

Career Aspirations

My goal is to work at the forefront of AI development, specifically in roles that involve human-AI interaction (HAI) or ethical system design. I aim to build products that serve as a natural extension of the human mind, bridging the gap between raw data and psychological reality.
